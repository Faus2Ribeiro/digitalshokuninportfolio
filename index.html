Digital Shokunin?

The word Shokunin comes from the Japanese 職人 for craftsman.
Since I mainly work digitally, I decided to create this identity of a Digital Shokunin.

I love to exercise ideas that exist in the depths of my mind through my skills on software like Photoshop, Premiere Pro and Fusion 360.
Specialized in Graphic Design, Product Design, UI/UX Design, Character and Environment Design, and Audio and Video Design.
I have other skills involving Photo Editing, Storyboarding and Planning for shoots, and basic HTML and CSS knowledge.
