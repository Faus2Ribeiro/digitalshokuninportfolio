Digital Shokunin?

The word Shokunin comes from the Japanese 職人 for a craftsman.
Since I mainly work digitally, I decided to create this identity of a Digital Shokunin.
I love to exercise ideas that exist in the depths of my mind through my skills on software like Photoshop, Premiere Pro and Fusion 360. Specialized in different areas of Design like Graphic Design and Product Design, and Audiovisual Multimedia. I have other skills involving Photo Editing, Storyboarding, and basic HTML and CSS knowledge.
