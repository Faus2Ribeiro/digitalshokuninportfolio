Videography

When I was a kid, I used to get hooked on LEGO stopmotions that I watched on Youtube. From then on, I wanted to bring things to life. I developed a love for animation and a few years later for cinematography.

The works that I show here, are only small projects that I've worked on.

Our World

Our world is threatned with forest fires, global warming, earthquakes, and the list goes on and on. In this video, I illustrate some of these problems in action, through the lens of a security camera. This is the result of my first try in stopmotion.

The Human Being of the Future

How will our sucessors from hundreds of years from now be? Due to reasons that threathen the livelihood of our planet, the human shape will change. In this project, I got together with my partner to create this video installation of human beings with spikes coming from their arms.
