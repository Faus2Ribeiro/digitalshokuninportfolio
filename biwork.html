2D Work

I've been drawing since I started my specialized training in Arts in highschool back in 2014.

I used to be terrible. I had no notion of the basics, and I thought on giving up after one year in that specialized training. Although, something in me changed and I kept on practising and by the end of my training, I aced the final exams and became one of the top scorers in my class.

Quite the underdog story, right? To this day, I'm still very proud of that achivement and it really shows that hard work can overcome everything.

I've been drawing seriously since 2014 but I feel like I only started to show my best work around 2017, when I entered university.

Here, I highlight the artworks that I consider to be the best I've ever created.
