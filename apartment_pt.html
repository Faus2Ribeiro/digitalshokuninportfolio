Small Model Apartment

I created this very small apartment in Fusion 360.
It's about 30 square meters big and it has a living room with a small kitchen cabinet (with a lot storage space), a big fridge and space to laid down a bed. It as a big closet in the small hallway and a bathroom with a built-in bath and a bathroom sink.
This was only a small, two day project. There are a lot of features missing like a toilet in the bathroom, but nonetheless I enjoyed creating it! Next time I give myself two days to work on a project like this, I'll make sure to not let these important features be overlooked.
