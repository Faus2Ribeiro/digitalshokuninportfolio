Dust Tech Gear

This was my final project in college. The assignment was to create a speculative design of a shelter to be used in 2050.
My group decided to study dust storms and how they can become a bigger threat in the future. We came up with a sleek design for a protective smart helmet that includes easy breathable filters, which clear all dust particles from getting inside; augmented reality compability with Google Maps and a lot more features. If youd like to learn more about our Dust Tech Gear, then come on over to our Wikifactory page.
