Presidency Room Proposal

This was my final assignment for my second year in college. With two other colleagues, we add to redesign the presidencial room of Lisbon School of Education, because the original room wasn't optimized for meetings.
My group came up with the proposal of adding a 10 seat meeting table, alongside a few storage cabinets, a white board to use during the meetings and a projector. Lastly we added an informal space for a smaller number of people to use. We decided that in our proposal we would paint the ceiling black and make it feel smaller with big lamps.
