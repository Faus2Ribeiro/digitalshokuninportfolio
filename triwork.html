Veet's EasierWax Epilator

For this project, I had to create a woman's epilator following the principles of Good Design by Dieter Rams. It was quite challenging, but alongside my partner, we came up with a user-friendly and sleek design for a wireless epilator, with a portable charger that includes dispensers for the wax slabs.
