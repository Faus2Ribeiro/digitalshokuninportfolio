Tower Stool

At Lisbon Schoold of Education, third year students have an assignment to create a stool for the Banco de Benfica brand, a brand created by Benfica's Fablab . I was tasked to create a stool that represented the area of Benfica. I wanted to create something outside of the box and that could reference the nature side of Benfica.
In the end, I decided to focus on the Monsanto Park towers, built out of wood. After sketching out my ideas, I started working in Fusion360 to create a parametric design. At the end of the project, I got to print out the stool in Benfica's Fablab using Valchromat material.
